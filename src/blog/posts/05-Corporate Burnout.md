If you feel constantly exhausted, disengaged, or emotionally detached from your role, your mental health may be signaling distress.

## What Is Corporate Burnout?

Corporate burnout is emotional, mental, and physical exhaustion caused by chronic workplace stress.

### The Three Stages of Burnout

1. **Overdrive** – Hyper-productive, high energy, saying yes to everything
2. **Depletion** – Irritability, declining enthusiasm
3. **Detachment** – Emotional numbness, “I don’t care anymore” mindset

By stage three, your mental health is already impacted.

---

## Early Warning Signs You Shouldn’t Ignore

### Mental Fatigue Despite “Normal” Work Hours

You log off but never mentally switch off.

### Declining Motivation

Work feels heavier, even if responsibilities haven’t changed.

### Emotional Withdrawal

You participate less. You engage less. You care less.

These are not personality flaws. They are stress management red flags.

---

## How to Prevent Corporate Burnout

### Set Strategic Boundaries

- No email after fixed hours
- Protect focus blocks
- Take actual lunch breaks

Boundaries protect mental health without sacrificing performance.

### Redefine Productivity

Not everything urgent is important. Prioritize impact over activity.

### Weekly Reset Ritual

- **Monday:** Top 3 priorities
- **Wednesday:** Energy check
- **Friday:** Reflection and boundary reset

This structure improves resilience and protects employee wellbeing.
