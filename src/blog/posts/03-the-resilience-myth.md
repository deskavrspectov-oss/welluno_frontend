## The Gaslighting of "Resilience"

In modern offices, "Resilience" has become a buzzword. But too often, it’s used as a shield for systemic failure. When a team is drowning under an impossible workload, the solution shouldn't be "resilience training." It should be workload reduction.

### The Band-Aid Effect

A meditation app is a tool, not a cure. If the environment you return to after 10 minutes of Zen is still high-pressure and low-support, the "peace" disappears before you even finish your next email.
